About Me

I live in Orlando Florida and live with my wife Kim and daughter Emily. I enjoy traveling and playing music. I love to listen to audio books and watch television shows about history. I play guitar and am trying my hand at archery. Learning and enjoying new things is important.


Originally from Clovis New Mexico, I have lived in Florida for most of my life. I just graduated from the University of Florida with an MBA in April of 2017. I plan on transitioning my career in the near future.
